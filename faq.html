Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Common Gathering

How do I join my church on Common Gathering?

Download the app, create an account, and search for your church by name. Once approved by your church admin, you’ll have full access.

Is the app free for church members?

Yes! The app is completely free for all church members. Churches pay a simple monthly subscription to have their own private community space.

Can I use it on both iPhone and Android?

Yes, Common Gathering is available on both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.
