Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy (herein referred to as “Policy”) explains how Common Gathering LLC (herein referred to as the “Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) processes Personal Data that we collect from you (herein referred to as the “Subscriber”) as a Controller.

1. DEFINITIONS

(a) “Controller” means the natural or legal person, public authority, agency, or other body, which alone or jointly with others, determines the purposes and means of processing Personal Data.

(b) “Personal Data” means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person (“data subject”). An identifiable natural person is one who can be identified, directly or indirectly, in particular by reference to an identifier such as a name, an identification number, location data, an online identifier, or to one or more factors specific to the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social identity of that natural person.

(c) “Service(s)” means a community engagement platform for churches and faith-based organizations, offering posts, events, groups, messaging, prayer requests, business listings, and administrative tools for managing members and content.

(d) “Subscriber” means the natural or legal person who has subscribed to the Service(s) by agreeing to the Terms.

2. DATA COLLECTION

The Subscriber directly provides the Company with Personal Data when using the Service(s). The Company may also automatically collect certain information about the Subscriber’s device and usage of the Service(s).

3. USE OF PERSONAL DATA

The Company uses Personal Data to provide and improve the Service(s), communicate with the Subscriber, and comply with legal obligations.

4. SHARING OF PERSONAL DATA

The Company shares Personal Data in the following instances: To provide hosting, analytics, authentication, payment processing (if applicable), fraud prevention, and other essential services required to operate the Common Gathering platform.

5. RETENTION OF PERSONAL DATA

The Company retains Personal Data when an ongoing legitimate business requires retention of such Personal Data. In the absence of a need to retain Personal Data, the Company will either delete or aggregate it.

6. SECURITY OF PERSONAL DATA

The Company uses appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the Personal Data it collects and processes.

7. MODIFICATION

The Company keeps this Policy under regular review and may update this webpage at any time. This Policy may be amended at any time, and the Subscriber shall be notified only if there are material changes to this Policy.

8. CONTACT DETAILS

If you have any concerns about this Policy, please get in touch with us at commongatheringapp@gmail.com.
