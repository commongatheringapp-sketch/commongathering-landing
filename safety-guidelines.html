Safety Guidelines

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Age Requirement: Common Gathering is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older.

At Common Gathering, we are committed to creating a safe, respectful, and Christ-centered community. These guidelines help ensure every member feels protected, valued, and able to engage meaningfully.

1. Respectful Communication

All interactions must reflect the love of Christ. Harassment, bullying, hate speech, discrimination, or abusive language will not be tolerated.

2. Content Guidelines

Do not post inappropriate, explicit, violent, or illegal content. Prayer requests and discussions should remain uplifting and appropriate for a church family.

3. Protecting Minors & Youth

Our platform is designed to be safe for all ages. Any content involving minors must follow strict safety standards. Suspicious or concerning behavior will be reported immediately.

4. Reporting Issues

If you see anything that violates these guidelines, please report it immediately through the in-app reporting feature or email us at commongatheringapp@gmail.com. We take every report seriously.

5. Moderation & Enforcement

Common Gathering LLC reserves the right to review, moderate, or remove any content or user that violates these guidelines. Repeated violations may result in account suspension or permanent ban.

6. Your Responsibility

By using Common Gathering, you agree to follow these safety guidelines and help maintain a healthy, loving community.

Questions? Contact us at commongatheringapp@gmail.com
