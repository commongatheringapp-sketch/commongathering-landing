Safety Guidelines

Last updated: May 2026

At Common Gathering, we are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and Christ-centered digital space for your church family. These guidelines help keep our community healthy and focused on connection, prayer, and ministry.

1. Age Requirement

Common Gathering is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older.

2. Respectful Communication

All posts, comments, prayer requests, messages, and group interactions must be kind, encouraging, and respectful. We do not allow harassment, bullying, hate speech, spam, or offensive language.

3. Prayer Requests & Privacy

Prayer requests are a sacred part of our community. Please only share information you are comfortable having visible to your church family. Mark sensitive requests as “Do Not Share” when appropriate.

4. Appropriate Content

Content must align with a Christian community environment. We do not permit graphic, explicit, political, or commercial content unrelated to church life and ministry.

5. Reporting Issues

If you see something that violates these guidelines, please report it immediately using the in-app report feature or contact us at commongatheringapp@gmail.com. All reports are reviewed promptly.

6. Consequences

Violations may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent removal from the platform at the discretion of church admins or the Common Gathering team.

7. Questions or Concerns

If you have any questions about these guidelines or need assistance, please reach out to your church administrator or email us at commongatheringapp@gmail.com.

Common Gathering is a tool to help churches stay connected. We rely on church leaders and the community to help maintain a safe and loving environment online.
